About Me

My name is Ben Harris and I am a 20-year-old Editor and Visual Effect Artist with a passion for Content Creation, 3D Design and Drone Cinematography. I am a 2020 Graduate of South Seas Film School where I received a Diploma in Film and TV Production with the major of Post Production. I am proficient in many software packages such as Avid Media Composer and the Creative Cloud Suite. I am a Self Taught Blender Artist where I try to create abstract and photorealistic scenes or environments. I aim to tackle every project with a positive hard-working attitude and to complete it at a high standard.
