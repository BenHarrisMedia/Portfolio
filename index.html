About Me

My name is Ben Harris and I am currently a Post Production Student at South Seas Film School. I am a 2019 Graduate of Dilworth School where I achieved an excellence endorsement for NCEA Level 3. I am proficient in many software packages such as Avid Media Composer and the Creative Cloud Suite. I am a Self Taught Blender Artist where I try to create abstract and photorealistic scenes or environments. I aim to tackle every project with a positive hard-working attitude and to complete it at a high standard.
